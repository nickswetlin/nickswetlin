Neural Network Capstone

  • Featured Skills

A six-month exploration on research behind Neural Network Test-Time Adaptation, a domain I originally knew nothing about -- we then presented our findings to students and industry professionals! In order to make our topic more accessible, we turned our presentation into a game: "Is This A Bird Or A Car?" -- this showcased the human ability to adapt on the fly surprisingly well, and helped others understand that machines -- thanks to our findings -- could get better at doing the same.
